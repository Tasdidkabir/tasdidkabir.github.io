Bateman ranks in a class of his own, with over 20 years of experience selling luxury homes and an exemplary track record of success.

And though he can hide his cold gaze, and you can shake his hand and feel flesh gripping yours, and maybe you can even sense your and his lifestyles are probably comparable, He simply is not there.
