13870 MULHOLLAND DR.



This grand architectural estate, located on the famous Mulholland Corridor, is privately perched on top of an acre of lush park-like grounds and boasts amazing panoramic canyon views from nearly every room. Enter through the formal foyer with gorgeous details throughout including exquisite crown molding, soaring 16 ft. vaulted ceilings, and dramatic high arched walkways, into two oversized living rooms with custom cut stone flooring and marble fireplaces. An impressive chef's kitchen includes Sub-Zero & Miele appliances and custom built-ins. Experience quintessential California indoor/outdoor living on the main level via beautiful French doors that access the spacious wrap-around balcony overlooking the huge grassy yard and stunning pool, multiple covered seating areas, outdoor BBQ, and a gas fire pit - perfect for

entertaining and relaxing. Follow the elegant staircase upstairs to the large primary suite replete with a jet tub, his and hers walk-in closets, minibar, and private balcony. 3 additional guest bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms complete the upstairs, with an additional maid's room on the main level. A second oversized primary suite that opens out to the backyard can be found downstairs along with a media room, game room, office, and wine cellar. Additional outdoor amenities include the sparkling pool, spa, waterfall, outdoor bath, cabana, and sports court. Suited for the most discerning occupant and entertainer, the gated motor court and parking area provides room for over 20+ cars, with an additional attached 2-car garage. A truly unique offering considering the home's size, scale, indoor/outdoor amenities, picturesque views, parking, and yard space.
